# Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM) – NovaPay Fintech

##  Overview
This project demonstrates a practical implementation of a Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM) framework aligned with ISO 27001 principles.

It focuses on identifying, assessing and mitigating risks associated with third-party vendors in a fintech environment.

---

##  Objectives
- Assess vendor security posture  
- Identify and evaluate third-party risks  
- Apply risk scoring methodology (Likelihood × Impact)  
- Recommend mitigation strategies  

---

##  Project Deliverables

-  TPRM Methodology  
-  Vendor Risk Assessment (Excel)  
-  Vendor Due Diligence Checklist  
-  TPRM Executive Report  

---

##  Key Concepts Demonstrated
- Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM)  
- Vendor Risk Assessment  
- Due Diligence & Security Questionnaires  
- Risk Scoring & Analysis  
- ISO 27001 Supplier Security Controls  

---

##  Risk Assessment Approach
Vendors were assessed based on:

- Data sensitivity  
- Business criticality  
- Likelihood of risk occurrence  
- Impact of potential incidents  

Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

---

##  Key Findings
- High-risk vendors identified in payment processing and cloud services  
- Gaps in encryption, monitoring, and compliance  
- Need for stronger vendor governance and oversight  

---

##  Recommendations
- Enforce Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)  
- Conduct periodic vendor audits  
- Implement continuous monitoring  
- Strengthen contractual security requirements  
- Ensure compliance certifications (ISO 27001, SOC 2)
